Unable to find valuables, robbers gangrape minor
Lucknow: A 12-year-old girl was gangraped when a dozen armed dacoits stormed into the house of a carpenter and failed to get anything valuable. The police reached the crime scene after four hours and senior officers reportedly after eight hours of the incident in the capital city. On the basis of the minor rape survivor's version and her father's account, the police have lodged an FIR against seven unidentified persons for dacoity, rape, and relevant sections of the POCSO Act. The police are yet to arrest anyone. Ram Kumar, IG, Special Task Force, said the case would be probed by them. The incident occurred at Salempur Pataura village on the outskirts of Lucknow. tns
