Sensex rose over 150 points and 50-share Nifty moved above its crucial psychological level of 8,800 on the back of broad-based buying amid strong global cues.The Sensex rose as much as 183 points to hit intraday high of 28,475 and Nifty rose 55 points to hit high 8,801.Buying was seen across the sectors with oil & gas, realty, auto, banking and capital goods indices advancing 0.7-1 per cent each.

From the Nifty basket of shares, 42 were declining while 9 were declining.Eicher Motors was among the top Nifty gainers, the stock advanced 2.8 per cent to Rs 26,513. ONGC, Kotak Mahindra Bank, Reliance Industries, Bajaj Auto, Coal India, Hero MotoCorp and GAIL India were also among the gainers, up 1-2.4 per cent each.On the other hand, Bharti Airtel, Hindalco, Wipro, Tata Power and Tech Mahindra were among the notable losers.The broader markets were trading firmly higher. The BSE mid-cap and small-cap indices advanced 0.7 and 0.9 per cent each respectively.Rupee opens higher at 66.44 per dollar against Wednesday's close of 66.46.Sensex and Nifty are set to open higher in trades today tracking firm global cues.The Nifty futures traded on the Singapore Stock Exchange also indicated a positive start for the Indian markets.
