4 of family beaten to death in Bareilly
Lucknow: A special task force has been handed over the investigation of the murder of four members of a family in Bareilly. The incident reportedly occurred two days ago but was discovered on Wednesday. When neighbours at the Veer Sarvarkar locality did not see any family member of insurance agent Naresh Sethi, they informed the police, which on Wednesday broke open the house. They found the bodies of 42-year-old Naresh, his 40-year-old wife Shikha, 15-year-old son Shubh and 14-year-old daughter Astha, who had been beaten to death with lathis. tns
