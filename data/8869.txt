Bangor pedestrian seriously injured after being hit by car
Police at the scene of the crash in upper Bangor

A pedestrian was rushed to hospital with serious injuries after being hit by a car in Bangor .

Shortly after 12.15pm police were called to Holyhead Road near the city's university after receiving reports a man had been struck by a vehicle .

The Welsh Ambulance Service sent an ambulance to the scene at 12.20pm and a man was taken to Ysbyty Gwynedd with serious, but not life-threatening, injuries.

The road was closed until 2.45pm with North Wales Police asking motorists to avoid the area while investigations took place.

A spokesman for the ambulance service said: “We were called at about 12.20pm to reports of a road traffic collision involving a car and a pedestrian on Holyhead Road in Bangor.

“A rapid response vehicle and an emergency ambulance attended the scene and a man was taken to Ysbyty Gwynedd with injuries which are not thought to be life-threatening.”

North Wales Police confirmed they were called at 12.16pm and a man had suffered serious injuries.
