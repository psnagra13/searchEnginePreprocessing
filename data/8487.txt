Oil firms and miners boost FTSE 100

(Open): London's leading shares opened strongly on Thursday, rising nearly 1%.

In the opening minutes, the benchmark FTSE 100 index gained 66.26 points to 6,915.64.

Oil and mining stocks dominated the list of winners, with Royal Dutch Shell adding 4.8% and Anglo American up 4.5%.

The biggest faller on the index was Alton Towers owner Merlin Entertainments, which slid 4.2% after reporting that visitor numbers at the theme park were still recovering.
