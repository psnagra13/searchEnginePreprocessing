Pakistani troops on Thursday violated the ceasefire along the LoC in Naugam sector of Kashmir by opening indiscriminate fire towards Indian Army posts.

“Pakistani troops resorted to unprovoked firing on Indian posts in Naugam sector at around 7 am,” an Army official said. He said the Pakistani troops used small fire arms and mortars but there were no reports of any casualty.
