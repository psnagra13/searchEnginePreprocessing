PM Narendra Modi To Review Most Favoured Nation Status To Pakistan
PM Narendra Modi will discuss whether to withdraw Most Favoured Nation (MFN) status to Pakistan

New Delhi: As he takes a series of steps to isolate Pakistan after the Uri terror attack, Prime Minister Narendra Modi will meet top officials today for discussions on whether to downgrade the country as a trading partner by withdrawing Most Favoured Nation (MFN) status.
