Reversing its three-day losing momentum, gold price recovered by Rs 50 to Rs 31,350 per 10 grams on Wednesday, with positive global cues doing the job amid scattered buying by jewellers at the domestic spot market.Silver also surged by Rs 400 to Rs 45,550 per kg on increased offtake by industrial units and coin makers.Traders said mild buying by jewellers at the domestic spot market, apart from a firm global trend on a weakening dollar, led to the recovery in the precious metal prices.
