Honda boss : We want a fast decision on Brexit
Following the UK's vote for Brexit, Japanese car manufacturer Honda has reaffirmed its commitment to the UK as a major manufacturing base, and says that Swindon-built Honda Civics would continue to be exported globally.

Speaking to the BBC's Theo Leggett at the Paris Motor Show, Honda's general manager for Europe, Jean Marc Streng, said the firm wanted "a fast decision" on Brexit.
