CBI arrests senior Civil Judge in bribery case
The CBI has arrested a senior judge of Tis Hazari court while allegedly accepting a bribe of Rs 4 lakh from a lawyer.

CBI sources said Rachna Tiwari Lakhanpal, Civil Judge, Tis Hazari court, was arrested while allegedly accepting a bribe of Rs 4 lakh from an advocate, who was appointed as local commissioner by her in a case she was adjudicating.

They said it is alleged that Rs 4 lakh was the first instalment of total demand of Rs 20 lakh demanded by her.
