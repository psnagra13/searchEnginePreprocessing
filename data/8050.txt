Now, tax-free liquor production in Bihar
Patna: Despite CM Nitish Kumar's cry for nationwide prohibition of liquor, his government has exempted all taxes on manufacturing, bottling and exporting for liquor units of the dry state. The state government has also extended the tax benefits to those who are likely to set up their liquor plants in the state, even though the illegal liquor imports from neighbouring states are at pick shown illegal liquor seizure by the state police.
