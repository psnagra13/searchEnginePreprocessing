Abduction police: 'Please check your dashcam footage'
Police appealing for information after a teenage girl walking to school was abducted by two men and raped are asking people to check dashcam and helmetcam footage for images of a silver car.

The girl, who was wearing a school uniform, was driven away at 08:25 BST on Wednesday and found more than three hours later about a mile away, knocking on doors and seeking help.
